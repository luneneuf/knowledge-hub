## 소스 개요

S&OP 개념의 창시자 Tom Wallace 본인이 진행한 OMTEC Chicago 워크샵 슬라이드 91페이지. **Implement·SCI는 모두 이 사람을 핵심 권위로 인용한다.** 그 1차 자료가 이 문서다.

**핵심 주장 (3가지):**
> 1. *"S&OP는 ABC다 — A=People, B=Data, C=Computer. 사람이 가장 크다."* (p75)
> 2. *"Build it and they will come은 낮은 성공 확률. 실은 Hold the high ground — 처음부터 top management를 끌어들여라."* (p84)
> 3. *"Implementation은 8-12개월, $100K 미만, 수십 명만 있으면 된다. Bad news: Change. Good news: Few People, Early Results, Low Cost."* (p72)

**기존 raw `wallace_sop_handbook_summary.md`와의 관계:** 같은 저자의 책 *"Sales & Operations Planning: The How-To Handbook"* 요약은 웹 리서치 기반(2차 자료)이며 일반론적. **이 워크샵 슬라이드는 1차 자료**이며 구체 다이어그램·예시·체크리스트가 풍부.

## 섹션별 핵심 내용

### Part 1. The Four Fundamentals — Volume vs Mix (p7-8)

Wallace의 가장 독특한 프레임워크. S&OP를 **2개의 직교 축**으로 분리:

|  | Volume | Mix |
|---|--------|-----|
| 질문 | How Much? | Which Ones? |
| 핵심 | Rates / The Big Picture | Timing / Sequence / Details |
| 단위 | Product Families | Products / SKUs / Orders |
| 빈도 | Monthly / 18 months+ | Weekly / Daily / 0.5-6 mos |
| 초점 | Strategy / Policy / Risk | Tactics / Execution |
| 책임 | **Top Management** | **Middle Management** |
| 프로세스 | **Executive S&OP** | **Operational S&OP** (Master Scheduling, DRP, MRP, Kanban, APS) |

→ "Sales & Operations Planning"이라는 용어 자체를 X 표시하고 **Demand × Supply / Volume × Mix** 4분면으로 재정의 (p7).

### Part 2. Where Executive S&OP Fits (p13-14)

```
Strategic Planning (3-5 years, Long Range)
        ↓
Business Planning (Annual $$$ Plan, Dollars, Ownership)
        ↓
Executive S&OP (units AND $$$, rolling monthly check on Business Plan)
        ↓
Detailed Planning, Scheduling & Execution
```

→ Business Plan과 Detailed Execution 사이의 **disconnect**를 메우는 게 Executive S&OP의 본질 (p17).

### Part 3. Executive S&OP Definition (p10-12, 26)

- 임원 의사결정 프로세스
- 수요·공급 균형
- units AND $$$ 동시 다룸
- **운영 plan과 재무 plan을 연결: "one set of numbers"**
- Risk management facilitates
- 전략·정책 설정의 forum
- 4개 인풋 reconcile: Sales/Marketing(Units/$$$ by Family) + Finance(Dollars) + Operations(Units/hours/Material) + Product Development(NPI Issues)
- 중심 = President/CEO/COO/GM (직접 hands-on)

### Part 4. The Role of Top Management — President 1.5 Hours/Month (p20-26)

President는 다음 3가지 hands-on:
1. **Stewardship** — 프로세스 자체의 정당성 부여
2. **Leadership** — Break ties, Set high standards, Motivate
3. **Decision-making** — 4개 인풋 reconcile 자리 주재

→ **월 1.5시간만 투자하면 됨**. 이게 S&OP의 가장 큰 ROI 포인트 (p25).

### Part 5. Hard Benefits / Soft Benefits (p27-31)

| Hard Benefits (정량) | Soft Benefits (정성) |
|----------------------|------------------------|
| Customer Service ↑ | Enhanced Teamwork |
| Inventory ↓ | Improved Communications (institutionalized) |
| Obsolescence ↓ | Better Decisions, Less Effort/Time |
| Freight Costs ↓ | Better $$$ Plans, Less Effort/Time |
| Order Lead Times ↓ | Greater Accountability |
| Supplier Lead Times ↓ | Greater Control |
| Time to Launch New Products ↓ | Window into the Future |
| Plant Productivity ↑ | **Top Management's Handle on the Business** |

### Part 6. Display of S&OP Information — Sally Smith's Spreadsheet (p32-38)

**Wallace의 가장 중요한 실무 자산.** 1페이지 표준 표:

```
            FEB  MAR  APR  MAY  JUN  JUL  AUG  SEP
FORECAST    100  100  100  100  120  120  120  120
ACTUAL SALES 90   95   85
DIFFERENCE  -10   -5  -15
CUM DIFF    -10  -15  -30

PLANNED PROD 100 100  100  110  120  120  120  120
ACTUAL PROD   98 100  101
DIFFERENCE    -2   0   +1
CUM DIFF      -2   -2   -1

PLANNED INV  100 100  100   142
ACTUAL INV   111 116  132
DIFFERENCE   +11  +16  +32
```

→ 3개 블록 (수요·공급·재고) 각각 Forecast / Actual / Difference / Cum Difference 추적. **케이스 "Bad Day at Acme Widget"**에서 Product Manager가 forecast 하향 조정 → Plant Manager가 production 조정 → CFO가 inventory 검토 → S&OP가 통합하는 흐름을 1페이지로 시연 (p33-38).

### Part 7. The 5-Step Executive S&OP Process (p41-42, 49-51)

```
Step 1: Data Gathering           (End of Month → Sales Actuals + Statistical Forecasts + Supply Actuals)
   ↓
Step 2: Demand Planning          (→ Management Forecast + 1st-pass spreadsheets)
   ↓
Step 3: Supply Planning          (→ Resource Requirements Plan + Capacity Constraints + 2nd-pass spreadsheets)
   ↓
Step 4: Pre-Meeting              ★ "Heavy Lifting" — Conflict Resolution + Recommendations + Scenarios + Agenda for Exec Meeting
   ↓
Step 5: Executive Meeting        (→ Decisions & Updated Game Plan)
```

**Timetable** (working days of month, p52):
- Day 1-3: Data Gathering
- Day 3-7: Demand Planning
- Day 4-10: Supply Planning
- Day 13: Pre-Meeting
- Day 15: Exec Meeting

**Sample Agenda — Exec Meeting** (p47):
1. Macro $$$ Review — All Families Combined
2. Customer Service Performance — Order Fill
3. New Product Issues
4. Family-by-Family Review and Decisions
5. Production/Procurement Rate Changes
6. Collective Impact on Business Plan
7. Recap of Decisions Made
8. Critique of Meeting

**Exec Meeting Participants** (p48):
- **Essential**: SBU Leader (Pres/GM), VP Sales, VP Marketing, VP Operations/Logistics, VP Product Development, VP Finance
- **Probable**: Exec S&OP Process Owner, Pre-Meeting Facilitator, Demand Manager, Supply Chain Manager
- **Possible**: Controller, Customer Service Mgr, Forecast Analyst, HR Mgr, Master Scheduler, Plant Mgr, Product Dev Mgr, Product Mgr, Purchasing Mgr

**Alternative Scenarios — Pre-Meeting 분석 예시** (p46):
> "Indiana plant overloaded, can't meet newly-increased demand. Alternatives: a) Add shift, b) Offload to Utah plant, c) Outsource, d) Combination — 각각 Cost Issues + 시간/품질/리드타임/유연성 평가"

### Part 8. Risk Management — 21st Century Supply Chain (p55-58)

**Supply Chain 특성**:
- Extended (확장됨)
- Brittle (취약함)
- Longer lead times
- More variable lead times

**4가지 Disruption 유형**:
1. Supply crash
2. Demand spike
3. Demand crash
4. Supply spike

**Anticipation (사전)**: hedges 설정 — safety stock, safety time, location, consignment, finished products, components/raw materials. Pre-Meeting과 Exec Meeting의 **상시 어젠다 항목**.

**Recovery (사후)**:
- Minor disruption: 역할 없음
- Moderate~Major disruption: **"Mini-S&OP Cycle"** 가동
  - 영향받은 부분만 focus
  - 가능한 가장 이른 단계에서 해결 (Step 2 Demand부터)
  - **같은 사람·포맷·프로세스 사용** (별도 비상 프로세스 X)

### Part 9. Global Executive S&OP — 7-Step (p61-69)

**The Global Challenge** (Percy Barnevik, ABB 전 CEO 인용):
> "We are a series of organizations doing business locally, with intense global coordination."

**구조**:
- **Entities** = 수요와 공급이 정렬되는 지역(예: NA = Mexico/US 공장 + 시장, Europe = Belgium 공장 + 시장)
- 각 Entity 내에서 5-Step 프로세스 완전 실행 (Exec Meeting까지)
- **Step 6: Global Consolidation** (world HQ에서)
- **Step 7: Global Exec Meeting** (주로 — 그러나 배타적이지 않음 — 재무 중심)
- Inter-entity coordination은 Demand Planning + Supply Planning + Pre-Meeting 단계

**예외**: 전 세계 판매 + 단일 공급 지역인 경우 → Global S&OP 필요 없음 (Entity 1개만 운영, p67)

### Part 10. Implementation — ABC of Implementation (p72-79)

**Bad News** | **Good News**
--- | ---
Change | Few People (수십 명)
8-12 Months | Early Results (2-3 Months)
| Low Cost (<$100K)

**ABC 우선순위 (역순으로 표시 = 중요도순)**:
- **A = People** ← 가장 큰 issue. Discipline / Self-discipline / Accountability / Conflict Management. Organizational behavior change.
- **B = Data** ← Families & Sub-families, Resources, Units of Measure, Load Profiles, Avg Selling Prices, Actuals, Demand/Supply Strategies (MTS/MTO/FTO, Target Fill Rates, Days On Hand)
- **C = Computer** ← 가장 마지막 우선순위

**Software 선택**:

| 옵션 | 장점 | 단점 |
|------|------|------|
| 1. Spreadsheet | Ultra low cost, Quick, No learning curve | Simulation slow, Unit/$$$ alignment weak, Volume/Mix alignment weak, "Blood Sweat & Excel", Dead End |
| 2. S&OP-specific software | Simulation rapid, Strong alignment, Fast preparation, Supports growth | Slows implementation, Cost, Learning curve |
| 3. **Hybrid** (start with spreadsheet, switch to specific later) | — | — |

→ **"When in doubt, pick 3."** (p82)

**Implementation Path** (p86):
- Month 0: Executive Briefing → Go/No-Go #1
- Month 1: Kickoff Session
- Month 1-3: **Phase I — Live Pilot** → Go/No-Go #2
- Month 3-7: **Phase II — Expansion**
- Month 7-9+: **Phase III — Financial Integration**

→ **Low Risk, Low Cost, High Impact** (p86)

**Implementation Principles** (p85):
1. People are the A item
2. Implementation begins and ends with the **Leader of the business**
3. **"Hold the high ground"** — top management을 처음부터·끝까지 끌어들여야 성공 가능성 ↑
4. **"Build it and they will come"** — design first, mechanics 만들고 나서 top mgmt 설득 = **낮은 성공 확률**

### Part 11. Identifying & Fixing Broken Process (p87-89)

**S&OP가 "broken"인 신호**:
- Top management is not involved
- Teamwork has not visibly improved
- Meeting attendance is poor
- Focus is on mix and the short term (← Volume·Long-term이 본질)
- Conflict is not resolved
- Finger-pointing is not "off limits"
- Decisions are not made

**고치는 법**:
- Convene a group (executive staff 멤버 포함)
- Self-audit
- 핵심 질문: "President와 staff가 매월 참여하는가?"
  - YES → self-audit 결과대로 문제 해결
  - NO → **re-implementation 필요**

**Re-implementation 경로**:
- Top mgmt이 Executive Briefing 동의? → YES면 Implementation Path 진행
- NO면 "Outsider Audit" 동의? → 동의 시 audit 결과 + mini Executive Briefing → 한 product family에서 제대로 시작
- 둘 다 NO면 포기 X — 살려두며 점진 개선, executive staff 멤버 참여 유지

### Part 12. The Future of S&OP — Growth Factors (p90)

- Success breeds success
- Lean Manufacturing과 S&OP 결합
- Globalization
- 전통 제조업 외부 신규 사용자
- S&OP-specific software 진화
- Greater financial integration, power, utility
- Executive suite에서의 입지 확대
