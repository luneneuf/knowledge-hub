## 핵심 주장 (5/11 페이지 기반)

> *"Companies with mature S&OP processes testify to the predictability and transparency it brings to a business. While neither the process nor this revelation is new, the fact is that **two-thirds of businesses do not progress beyond the first two stages** of Gartner's 4-stage maturity model. It's in the mature Stage 3 and 4 where S&OP derives real business value."* (page 1)

→ **2009년 8월 182개 제조사·소매사 서베이 결과 67%가 Stage 1 또는 2에 정체**. 이게 후속 5-stage 진화 (Integrate 단계 추가)의 motivation.

---

## Key Findings (page 1 원문)

1. *"Beyond Stage 2, traditional S&OP methodology is no longer sufficient and the process must be tailored to the specific needs of an organization."*
2. *"In most organizations there was a compelling business event that precipitated the change from the top, and gave clarity to the S&OP journey and vision."*
3. *"Once the S&OP process matures, it is no longer owned by supply chain but by **business leaders**."*
4. *"Momentum builds as business leaders derive value from the process and it becomes the **primary forum for decision making**."*
5. *"Where the term S&OP was tainted, organizations renamed and rebranded the process."*

---

## Recommendations (page 1-2 원문)

1. Assess your level of S&OP maturity to build a road map
2. Have a clear, executive-led business motive and common business metrics
3. Identify coaches and provide tools to help sustain the process
4. Design the process to fit business nuances — consider multi-tier approach
5. Determine right balance between process improvement and enabling technologies

## 원본 4-Stage Maturity Model (2010) — Figure 1

본 wiki [[gartner_5stage_maturity|gartner_5stage_maturity §2]] 정정 필요한 부분. Gartner 원본은 4-stage:

```
Stage 1: Reacting → Stage 2: Anticipating → Stage 3: Collaborating → Stage 4: Orchestrating
                              "Increase in Organizational Balance"
```

→ 현재 wiki에 명시된 5-stage (React → Anticipate → **Integrate** → Collaborate → Orchestrate)는 **2010년 이후 진화 버전**. 원본 (Barrett & Uskert 2010)에는 "Integrate" 단계 없음.

### 3 차원 × 4 단계 매트릭스

| 차원 | Stage 1 Reacting | Stage 2 Anticipating | Stage 3 Collaborating | Stage 4 Orchestrating |
|------|---------------------|------------------------|--------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Goals** | Development of an operational plan | Demand and supply matching | **Profitability** | **Demand sensing**, conscious trade-offs for demand shaping to drive optimized demand response |
| **Cross-Functional Alignment** | Supply Chain driven with strong sales or operational bias leading to imbalance. Lack of clarity as to the goal of S&OP | Supply Chain driven for purposes of achieving optimum forecast and supply response to demand | **Supply Chain becomes the S&OP orchestrator** and business functions take ownership of input, output and results, looking at financial impact of decisions | **Business ownership at multiple levels** with strong participation from executives and finance. **Collaboration extends beyond the enterprise to achieve end-to-end value** |
| **Process and Technology** | Emerging process, inconsistent and marginally effective. Often more of a sales review meeting. Tools mainly Excel and ERP | Formal, structured process. **One size fits all** approach. Tools extend to include forecasting, SC planning and inventory optimization | **Process tailored to business model and needs**. Dialogue, and start of use of tools, around **what-if analysis** for demand shaping, financial reconciliation and cost to serve | **Process becomes balanced, dynamic and event-driven**. Strong connection to strategic planning and execution. Tools also support **risk-value trade-offs, price optimization and complex simulation** |

→ **Source: Gartner (October 2010)**

## Stage 2 함정 — "Stuck in Neutral" (page 3)

> *"A good Stage 2 process typically follows the traditional S&OP demand/supply balancing methodology based on volume."*

**Stage 2 특징** (원문):
- Volume·units 데이터 기반
- Dialogue centers on volume, SKU capacity, lead times
- **S&OP ownership remains stuck in supply chain**
- Demand planners + Sales/Marketing customer/market intelligence 통합
- **FVA (Forecast Value Added) 측정** 시작 일부
- Pre-S&OP meetings로 demand→supply translation
- Strong Stage 2 = formal governance + good discipline

**Stage 2 한계**:
- Planning horizon이 0-3개월 단기에 머묾
- **S&OP-to-execution 연결 부재** ← Stage 3 진입의 핵심 갭

---

## Stage 3 진화 — "Collaborative" (page 3-4)

**Stage 3 정의** (원문):
- Planning horizon: **3-24 months midterm**
- Goal: maximize market opportunity, profitability, customer satisfaction while minimizing risk
- **Specifically designed and personalized** to business/regional needs
- Multi-tier process (global aggregated midterm + local operational short-term)
- One-size-fits-all 거부

**Stage 3 핵심 변화** (원문 인용):

> *"This is key, because once the process moves to Stage 3 **it is no longer a supply chain process, but rather an aligned business planning process**."*

> *"S&OP is **THE forum for decision making**, and business leaders embrace the meetings and actively participate."*

**유럽 S&OP 팀 인용** (원문):

> *"We realized our S&OP process was good when **people defer to it as the forum for decision making**. Business folks now value the process, the dialogue and opportunity to interact with peers and make good decisions."*

**Stage 3 기술 도구** (원문):
- Trade Promotion Management (TPM)
- Vendor-Managed Inventory (VMI)
- Collaborative Planning Forecasting and Replenishment (CPFR)
- Supplier portals + visibility tools

## Translation — Stage 2→3 핵심 능력 (page 4)

> *"One of the most challenging aspects of S&OP is the ability to satisfy the informational needs of different business functions (finance, sales, marketing, supply chain) based on input from multiple sets of numbers and forecasts. **At Gartner, we refer to this ability as Translation**."*

**Demand Translation 8 차원** (Figure 3 원본):

| 차원 | 의미 |
|------|------|
| SKU | 개별 상품 |
| Global Units | 글로벌 합산 단위 |
| Currency | 통화 환산 |
| Profit | 수익 환산 |
| Customer | 고객별 |
| Product Category | 카테고리별 |
| Plant | 공장별 |
| Countries / Regions | 국가·지역별 |

→ Stage 3 진입 = 8 차원 demand translation 자유롭게.

---

## Financial Integration — S&OP-AOP 6-Step (page 5) ⭐

**원문 인용**:

> *"In one company, the S&OP process has become so tightly integrated in the financial planning process that they are almost indistinguishable."*

**6-Step (실제 본문 a-h)**:

| Step | 활동 |
|------|------|
| (a) | Demand plan 생성·합의 (monthly demand-consensus) → AOP/budget·strategic planning base |
| (b) | Supply plan 계산: 완제품 재고·생산 plan·원자재/WIP 재고 |
| (c) | Constraints S&OP 프로세스에서 처리 |
| (d) | Volume이 manufacturing plants/supply channels로 흘러 costing 산출 |
| (e) | Inventory plans 확정 → cash flow 계산 |
| (f) | Inventory volume이 distribution channels에서 costing 산출 |
| (g) | SG&A budgets 확정 |
| (h) | Finance가 profit 산출. **Undesirable 시 step a-g 변경 trigger** |

→ Step (d)·(f)는 보통 **분기 단위** (월간 운영은 시간 부족).

**핵심 가치** (원문):

> *"This integration not only enables the organization to have a discussion on a monthly basis around the **risk-reward trade-offs of supply and demand**, but also enables discussion of profit impact and visibility into whether the company is tracking to its financial plan."*

---

## Profitability Discussion 정의 (page 5)

**Stage 3 vs Stage 4 profit 정의 차이**:

| Stage | Profit 정의 | 데이터 요구 |
|--------|------------|--------------|
| Stage 3 | **Gross profit** (sales price − COGS) | COGS만 — 상대적 용이 |
| Stage 4 진입 | **Total cost to serve** or **"Profitable to serve"** = Operating profit / EBIT | COGS + SG&A + customer-specific costs |

> *"Few companies have managed to efficiently collect and manage the data required to calculate cost-to-serve or do dynamic profit calculations beyond gross profit."*

→ Stage 4 진입 = **data infrastructure가 EBIT 단위로 운영**되어야.

## Stage 3 S&OP Governance Framework (Figure 2, page 5)

확보된 부분 (Definition 표):

| 영역 | 내용 |
|------|------|
| **Sponsor** | Senior business leaders, in some companies the **CEO** |
| **Coordinator** | Most commonly **Supply Chain**, occasionally Customer Service |
| **Owner** | **Line-of-business functions**, sometimes joint ownership of key S&OP components |
| **KPI focus** | Demand risk, customer service, profit, cash, market share |
| **Performance focus** | Ability to translate opportunities and demand into actionable and profitable response from make/source/deliver functions, understanding trade-offs and financial impact, conscious choice on decisions |

**Output focus**:
- 3-18/24 month planning horizon
- Consensus plans (demand, supply, inventory, product portfolio, mix, revenue)
- Demand shaping to close gaps to budget and profit targets
- Scenario analysis (financial impact of forecast risk, demand shaping, supply constraints, risks)
- Investment decisions (inventory, assets, resources)
- **S&OP demand plan becomes the AOP** + strategic planning starting point
- Plan + assumptions communicated to stakeholders
- **Connection to execution through aligned planning processes**

→ **Source: Gartner (October 2010)**

Stage 2→3 진화 필요 2 종 변화:

1. **Quantitative changes** — process or system-driven initiatives
2. **Qualitative changes** — change management, culture, outlook, fundamental beliefs
