### 1.4 EUROPE

**Market Overview**
- Europe lipstick: USD 4.54B (2024) → USD 5.35B (2030), CAGR 2.80%. [(Research and Markets)](https://www.researchandmarkets.com/report/europe-lipstick-market)
- Western Europe beauty +7.7% value growth in 2024

**Sustainability Demands**
- 54% of European consumers consciously purchased sustainable-packaged products in prior 6 months
- France's loi AGEC: brands must implement take-back or refill systems by 2025
- Refillable formats: YSL launched refillable system in France, Germany, Italy [(Global Cosmetics News, 2025)](https://www.globalcosmeticsnews.com/2025-beauty-industry-review-trends-tech-regulation/)

**EU Regulatory Impact on Consumer Choices**

| Regulation | Impact | Effective Date |
|---|---|---|
| Omnibus VII (CMR substances) | Bans carcinogenic/mutagenic compounds | Sep 1, 2025 |
| Alpha-arbutin removal | Must be removed from shelves | Nov 1, 2025 |
| PFAS ban (France) | Banned in cosmetics including lipsticks | Jan 1, 2026 |
| Fragrance allergen labeling (2023/1545) | 80+ allergens must be disclosed | In force 2023+ |
| Heavy metals | Lead <10 mg/kg, arsenic <2 mg/kg, cadmium <5 mg/kg | Ongoing |

[(EU Verify)](https://euverify.com/resource/upcoming-cosmetic-ingredient-bans-in-the-eu/); [(Cosmeservice)](https://cosmeservice.com/news/france-bans-pfas-eu-regulations/)

**"Clean beauty" in EU = regulatory compliance** (not just marketing language) — a key distinction from the US, where "clean" is self-defined.

## Cross-Regional Key Findings

1. **Hydration displaced pigmentation as #1 baseline expectation globally.** All four markets now treat moisturizing as table stakes.
2. **Korea → Japan → Global format pipeline.** Lip tint / blurred lip originated in Korea, spread to Japan (粘膜 adaptation), now drives TikTok globally as "Douyin lip."
3. **Japan is most review-platform-dependent.** @cosme and LIPS reviews drive purchasing more directly than any other regional platform.
4. **EU regulation reshaping European consumer language.** "PFAS-free," "allergen-disclosed," "refillable" are active purchase decision terms in France/Germany/Netherlands.
5. **TikTok's "lip combo" restructured US behavior.** Multi-product routines drove 28–60% growth in adjacent categories (liner, oil) YoY.
