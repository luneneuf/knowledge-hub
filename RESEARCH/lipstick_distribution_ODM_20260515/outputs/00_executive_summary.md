# Executive Summary: Lipstick Distribution Channels & ODM/OEM Manufacturing Landscape
*Research Date: May 2026 | Geography: Korea, Japan, USA, EU/Global*

---

## Key Findings at a Glance

### Distribution
- **Korea:** OliveYoung is the undisputed gatekeeper of Korean beauty retail, operating 1,350+ stores with KRW 4.8 trillion (USD 3.4B) in 2024 sales. Online now accounts for ~50% of all cosmetics sales. Musinsa Beauty GMV surged 50%+ YoY. International H&B rivals (Watsons/Lalavla, Sephora) have all exited Korea.
- **Japan:** @cosme's award system (17.6M MAU) functions as the primary brand credibility signal. Matsumoto Kiyoshi (3,400+ stores) dominates drugstore distribution. Department stores (Isetan, Mitsukoshi) anchor prestige. Online = 41.7% of market (Rakuten + Amazon Japan lead).
- **USA:** Sephora anchors prestige; Ulta bridges prestige/mass (36% YoY ad spend increase in 2025). Amazon Beauty & Personal Care is projected to reach USD 36B in 2024, up 26% YoY—the fastest-growing channel. DTC fell ~10% in 2024 as volume shifted to Amazon and mass retail.

### ODM Manufacturing
- **Korea Big Three:** Cosmax (2024 revenue ~USD 1.2B), Kolmar Korea (KRW 2.45 trillion, ~USD 1.7B), and Cosmetica Korea are the global leaders. Both Cosmax and Kolmar passed the KRW 2 trillion revenue mark in 2024.
- **Lipstick MOQ/Lead Time:** Cosmax: 3,000 units/shade (stock ODM), 9–11 week lead time. Kolmar: 5,000 units/shade, 7–9 weeks. Chinese ODM (Guangdong): from 1,000 units; 8–12 weeks. Italian (Intercos): 3,000–5,000 units, 12–16 weeks.
- **Cost comparison:** Korean ODM: USD 2.10–4.50/unit. Italian ODM: USD 3.80–9.50/unit. Chinese ODM: USD 1.85–3.40/unit.
- **Global ODM market** projected to grow from USD 16.5B (2026) to USD 30.5B (2036) at 6.4% CAGR.

### Regulatory
- **Korea (MFDS):** Korean Cosmetics Act requires Korean-language labeling, Responsible Distributor registration with KPTA, and pre-import notification. 1,032 prohibited ingredients; separate restricted lists for colorants, UV filters, preservatives. Functional cosmetics (UV, whitening) require pre-market MFDS approval.
- **USA (FDA):** Lip color = cosmetic under FD&C Act. Color additives require FDA approval (21 CFR Parts 73/74/82). D&C Red dyes requiring batch certification cannot contact mucous membranes (lip-safe restrictions apply). Lead limit: 10 ppm guidance (FDA Draft Guidance, December 2016, Federal Register Vol. 81, No. 246).
- **Japan (MHLW/PMD Act):** Plain lipstick = cosmetic (notification, no pre-approval). Lip products with efficacy claims = quasi-drug (pre-market approval required). Japanese-language labeling mandatory. Import takes 1–5+ months via Authorized Cosmetics Partner.
- **EU (Regulation 1223/2009):** Responsible Person required in EU territory; CPNP notification before market entry; Cosmetic Product Safety Report (CPSR) mandatory. Omnibus VIII (Reg. EU 2024/2564) bans CMR substances effective May 1, 2026. Fragrance allergen labeling expands to 80 allergens by July 31, 2026.
